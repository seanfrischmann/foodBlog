Four Layer Cookie Brownie Cake

Preparer: Sean Frischmann

Cookie Cake
• Melt two sticks of butter
• Mix in two large eggs
• Mix in 3/4 cup brown sugar
• Mix in 3/4 cup white sugar
• Mix in 1 Tbsp Baking Soda, Vanilla Extract, and Salt
• Mix in 2 and 1/4 cups flour
• Mix in 1 cup Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
• Bake at 350° for 15 mins

Brownie
• Add 2 eggs to a bowl
• Mix in 3/4 cup of white sugar
• Mix 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract
• Mix in 1/2 cup of melted butter
• Add 3/4 cup Cocoa
• Mix in 2/3 cup Flour
• Add 1/4 tsp Baking Powder
• Add 1/4 tsp salt
• Bake at 350° for 15 mins

Chocolate Icing
• Soften two sticks of butter and add to bowl
• While mixing add 1/2 cup confectionary sugar
• Add in 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
• Mix in 3/4 cup Cocoa
• Slowly mix in confectionary sugar to thicken
• Add more Cocoa to desired richness
Note: confectionary sugar and cocoa are estimates not exact, the trick is to add slowly until icing looks and feels right.

Review: Chocolate lovers dream, this cake turned out better than any I've made in the past! Hopefully it is repeatable.
